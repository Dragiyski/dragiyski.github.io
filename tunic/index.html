Help

Keys

F1-F6 modifies vowel lines, starting on the right, rotating counter-clockwise.
F7-F12 modifies consonant lines, starting on the right, rotating counter-clockwise..
Tab modifies the inverse modifier.
Enter submits the current symbol if not empty.
Ctrl+Enter starts a new line.

Phonemes

Vowels

  • æ as (a) in cat, or lack
  • aː as (long-a) in are, car, or arm
  • ɒ as (o) in swan, or top
  • eɪ as (alphabet-a) in pay, or say
  • ɛ as (e) in end, or send
  • iː as (long-i or ee) in bee, or see
  • ɪə as (ee) in beer, or here
  • ə as (e) in the (before consonant)
  • eə as (ei) in air, or heir
  • ɪ as (short-i) in sit, or bit
  • aɪ as (alphabet-i) in guy, or hi
  • ɜː as in , or as (ir) in bird, or dirt
  • əʊ as (oh) in toe
  • ɔɪ as (oi) in toy, or boi
  • uː as (oo) in too, or moo
  • ʊ as (u) in put, or look
  • aʊ as (ow) in how, or cow
  • ɔː as (long-o) in your
